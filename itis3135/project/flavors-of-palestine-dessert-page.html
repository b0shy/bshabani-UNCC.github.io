Dessert Recipes:

Knafeh

Syrup ingredients:
  • 4 cups of sugar
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon juice
Knafeh ingredients:
  • 1 package of shredded filo dough
  • 1 1/2 cups of melted unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup of crushed pistachio
  • 1/2 pound of fresh mozzarella
  • 1 pound of queso blanco
  • 1 tablespoon of ricotta cheese
  • Orange food coloring
Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 400° F
  2. In a food processor, chop the filo dough finely, then place it in a large bowl
  3. In another bowl, mix the ricotta, mozzarella, and queso blanco together
  4. Soak the bowl of cheese in water for 10 minutes to remove the salt, then wring out the water from the cheese
  5. Add 2 tablespoons of sugar to the bowl of cheese
  6. Melt the butter and mix in the food coloring with it
  7. Pour the melted butter into the bowl of dough, mix until the butter is absorbed
  8. Evenly spread the dough into a 10 inch circular pan and firmly press into the bottom and the edges
  9. Spread the cheese over the pan, avoiding the edges
  10. Bake until the cheese is melted and slightly golden brown and the edges of the dough are brown (around 30 minutes)
  11. For the syrup, combine the water and sugar in a small sauce pan and bring it to a boil
  12. After it reaches a boil, lower the heat and stir in the lemon juice
  13. Let simmer until the sugar is dissolved and the syrup has thickened
  14. Remove the pan from the oven
  15. Place a large plate over the entire pan and carefully flip it upside down
  16. Slowly lift up the pan, leaving the knafeh in the plate
  17. Lightly pour the syrup over the knafeh and cut it into sqaure pieces
  18. Garnish each piece with the crushed pistachio
  19. Enjoy!

Hareesah

Syrup ingredients:
  • 4 cups of sugar
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
Hareesah ingredients:
  • 4 1/2 cups of semolina
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 3/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup of whole plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Crushed pistachio
Directions:
  1. For the syrup, combine the water and sugar in a small sauce pan and bring it to a boil
  2. After it reaches a boil, lower the heat and stir in the lemon juice
  3. Let simmer until the sugar is dissolved and the syrup has thickened
  4. Preheat the oven at 350° F
  5. In a large mixing bowl, thoroughly mix all of the hareesah ingredients except for the tahini
  6. Spread the tahini on the large baking tray
  7. Pour the hareesah mixture into the baking tray and spread it out evenly
  8. Place the tray into the preheated oven
  9. Take the hareesah out of the oven when it is golden brown
  10. Pour the syrup over the hareesah while it is hot, then leave it on the counter to cool down
  11. Cut the hareesah into square pieces and garnish each piece with pistachio
  12. Enjoy!

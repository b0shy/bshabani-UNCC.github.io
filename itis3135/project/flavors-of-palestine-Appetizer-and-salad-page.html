Appetizer and Salad Recipes:

Hummus

Ingredients:
  • 16 ounces of washed chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup of tahini
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • Olive oil
  • 3 ice cubes
  • Paprika
Directions:
  1. In a food processor combine all of the ingredients except for the paprika
  2. Blend the ingredients and then while blending, slowly add water until the hummus is soft and fluffy, but not runny
  3. Adjust the lemon juice and salt to taste
  4. Place hummus on a serving plate and add olive oil and paprika for garnish
  5. Enjoy!

Baba Ghanoush

Ingredients:
  • 2 medium sized eggplants
  • 1/3 cup of tahini
  • 3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Parsley
Directions:
  1. Roast the eggplant in the oven
  2. Peel the eggplant and place it into a bowl and let it cool down to room temperature
  3. Add tahini, chopped garlic, lemon juice, salt, and one tablespoon of olive oil to the bowl
  4. Mash the everything in the bowl together
  5. Adjust the salt, lemon juice, and tahini to taste
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and garnish with parsley
  7. Enjoy!

Fattoush

Ingredients:
  • 2 pita bread cut into small 1 inch squares, fried until golden brown
  • 3 medium tomatoes chopped
  • 3 medium cucumbers, coarsely chopped
  • 1 romaine lettuce, cut into 3/4 inch strips
  • 1 cup of chopped parsley leaves
  • 1 bundle of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 large bell pepper, chopped
Dressing ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons of sumac spice
  • 4 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of pomegranate molasses
  • 2 small garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of dried mint
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • salt
Directions:
  1. Combine the dressing ingredients together, whisk until well mixed
  2. Mix the vegetables in a large bowl
  3. Add 3/4 of the dressing mix and toss salad until well coated
  4. Adjust dressing and salt to taste
  5. Add the fried pita bread and gently toss it into the salad
  6. Enjoy!

Tabouleh

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup of bulgur
  • 10 bunches of fresh parsley
  • 2 lemons
  • 3 large tomatoes
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 stems of green onions
  • olive oil
  • salt
Directions:
  1. Finely chop the parsley
  2. Cut the tomatoes into small cubes
  3. Finely chop the yellow and green onions
  4. Soak the bulgur in lemon juice for an hour
  5. Mix the vegetables with the soaked bulgur
  6. Add salt and olive oil
  7. Adjust the lemon juice and salt to taste
  8. Enjoy!

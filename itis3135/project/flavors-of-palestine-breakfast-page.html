Breakfast Recipes:

Shakshoukah

Ingredients:
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 fresh large tomatoes, finely diced
  • Parsley, finely chopped
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Directions:
  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan on medium heat
  2. Add chopped onions and stir until golden brown
  3. Add diced tomatoes and stir for 5 minutes
  4. Add eggs and mix in pan
  5. Add pepper and salt, season to taste
  6. Continue cooking on medium heat until eggs are done
  7. Add finey chopped parsley as garnsih
  8. Enjoy!

Manakeesh Zaatar

Ingredients:

Dough:
  • 6 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 2 cups of warm milk
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of instant yeast
Zaatar:
  • 10 tablespoons of zaatar
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
Directions:
  1. Put all of the dough ingredients in a large mixing bowl until the dough is soft and no longer sticky
  2. Form the dough into one large ball and leave it on the counter to rise for 1 hour
  3. Prepare the zaatar by mixing the zaatar and olive oil together in a bowl
  4. Preheat the oven to 350Â° F
  5. After 1 hour, divide the large dough ball into smaller dough balls, about the size of a tennis ball
  6. Flatten each dough ball into a circle with a 5 inch diameter
  7. Take the zaater you mixed earlier and apply a generous layer on the face of the dough
  8. Bake for 7-8 minutes or until dough is golden brown
  9. Enjoy!

Mfarakeh

Ingredients:
  • 3 large potatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
Directions:
  1. Peel potatoes and chop into small cubes
  2. Place the vegetable oil in a pot on medium to high heat
  3. Place the chopped potatoes into the pot and fry until golden brown
  4. Place the fried potatoes into a different pan and add eggs, salt, and pepper
  5. Mix contents of pan until eggs are done
  6. Serve with pita bread
  7. Enjoy!
